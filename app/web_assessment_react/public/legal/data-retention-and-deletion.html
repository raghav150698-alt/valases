Data Retention and Deletion

Product default for customer and legal review · Effective 19 July 2026

DataDefault
Temporary unsubmitted session30 days
Submitted answers, work, scores, reviews24 months
Proctor events and model signals12 months
Camera, audio, image, or video evidence30 days after review
Audit and security logs24 months
BackupsRolling 35 days

Deletion requests are verified, checked for legal holds, applied to database rows, object storage, exports, caches, and derivatives, and recorded in a minimal deletion audit. The organization issuing the assessment must finalize the schedule and contact details.
