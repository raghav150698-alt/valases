Data retention and deletion

Effective 19 July 2026

The following periods are the platform defaults. The organization issuing an assessment may apply a shorter period or retain information longer where required by law, dispute resolution, security investigation, or another documented obligation.

InformationDefault retention
Temporary, unsubmitted assessment session30 days
Submitted answers, work, scores, and reviews24 months
Proctoring events and model signals12 months
Camera, audio, image, or video evidence, if separately enabled30 days after review
Audit and security logs24 months
Encrypted backupsRolling 35 days

Deletion requests

Requests are verified and checked for legal or security holds. Approved deletion is applied to the relevant database records, stored files, exports, caches, and derived data according to the issuing organization's obligations. A minimal deletion audit may be retained to demonstrate completion.

How to make a request

Contact the organization that sent the assessment invitation. Include the email address used for the assessment and enough information to identify the relevant invitation.
