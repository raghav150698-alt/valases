Candidate Assessment and Proctoring Consent

Version 1.0 · Effective 19 July 2026

I understand that the organization that issued my assessment link may collect my answers, submitted files, formulas, code, timestamps, and assessment activity to administer, score, secure, and review this assessment.

If this assessment is proctored, I consent to the stated browser checks and any separately disclosed camera, microphone, recording, fullscreen, tab-visibility, focus, clipboard, restricted-shortcut, and model-derived integrity signals. Automated proctoring signals are not, by themselves, a final employment decision. I can contact the issuing organization about privacy, accessibility, accommodation, or review.
