Assessment and proctoring consent

Version 1.1 | Effective 22 July 2026

This notice applies when you choose to begin a proctored assessment delivered through Valases.

Assessment information

Your answers, submitted work, files, formulas, code, timestamps, and assessment activity are collected so the issuing organization can administer, score, secure, and review your assessment.

Integrity checks

The session may check fullscreen status, tab visibility, window focus, clipboard actions, and restricted browser shortcuts. With camera access, on-device analysis may produce attention, face-count, and prohibited-object signals, including mobile-phone detections. Camera frames are not recorded or uploaded by this flow.

Warnings and review

An integrity signal may pause the timer and display a warning. Leaving fullscreen or reaching the warning limit can close the assessment and submit the attempt for review. Signals and provisional score adjustments are visible to the recruiter and must be considered during human review. They are not, by themselves, a final employment decision.

Your choices

You can decline camera access and not begin the assessment. Contact the issuing organization before starting if you need an accommodation, an alternative assessment arrangement, or have a privacy concern.

Consent confirmationBy selecting the consent checkbox in the assessment, you confirm that you have read this notice and agree to the disclosed assessment processing and integrity checks.
