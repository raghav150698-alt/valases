Certora Privacy Policy

Draft for legal review · Effective 19 July 2026

Certora processes recruiter account data, assessment content, candidate answers and submitted work, scores, timestamps, technical security logs, and, where configured and consented, proctoring data such as camera or microphone evidence and browser integrity events.

Use

We use this information to authenticate users, deliver and score assessments, secure the assessment, support recruiter review, prevent abuse, and maintain the service. Automated proctoring signals are not by themselves a final employment decision.

Retention

Default retention periods are described in the retention and deletion policy. Candidates may contact the organization that issued the assessment for access, correction, deletion, accommodation, or review requests.

Security

Production deployments should use HTTPS, authenticated access, restricted storage, audit logs, and configured deletion jobs. Browser-only controls cannot prevent all screenshots or operating-system activity.

This is an implementation draft and must be completed by Certora's registered legal entity and counsel.
