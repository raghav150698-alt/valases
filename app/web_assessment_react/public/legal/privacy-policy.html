Privacy policy

Effective 19 July 2026

This policy explains how information is handled when an organization uses Valases to issue and review an assessment.

Information processed

  • Candidate identity and invitation details supplied by the issuing organization.
  • Answers, files, formulas, code, form entries, scores, timestamps, and reviewer decisions.
  • Technical and assessment-integrity events, such as fullscreen changes, focus changes, clipboard attempts, gaze signals, and prohibited-object signals where proctoring is enabled.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate candidates, deliver and score assessments, protect assessment integrity, support recruiter review, prevent misuse, and maintain the service. Automated integrity signals are supporting evidence for human review and are not, by themselves, a final employment decision.

Camera processing

When camera proctoring is enabled, camera frames are analyzed in the candidate's browser to produce attention and object-detection signals. This assessment flow does not upload or retain camera frames, audio, or video unless a different recording practice is expressly disclosed before consent.

Sharing and access

The organization that issued the assessment can access the candidate's submission, scoring evidence, and integrity events. Service providers may process limited information where needed to operate and secure the platform.

Retention and requests

Retention periods are described in the data retention and deletion policy. Candidates should contact the organization that issued the assessment to request access, correction, deletion, accommodation, or human review.

Questions about a specific assessment should be sent to the contact listed in the assessment invitation.
